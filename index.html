INVESTIGATOR CASE LOG – ROBLOXIA MISSING PERSONS

Date: April 7, 2026
Investigator: [Redacted]

I’ve been patrolling Robloxia and noticing a disturbing pattern—people aren’t just disappearing… they’re all vanishing near the same place. A forest locals have started calling the “Death Forest.”

Five children went missing first. No signs, no witnesses, just gone. Now adults have started disappearing too. The most notable case is Timmy, who was last seen heading toward the forest while investigating unusual activity in the area.

No one who enters that forest comes back. Not alive.

At first, I thought it was coincidence. It isn’t.

Multiple witnesses report seeing something standing just inside the trees at night. Always the same description: a dark figure with a glowing green smile. Some say they heard chains dragging along the ground. Others describe a low mechanical revving sound, like a chainsaw idling… waiting.

They all say the same thing:
It doesn’t leave the forest.

One witness claimed the figure stood completely still until someone stepped closer to the tree line. The moment they crossed into the forest, the sounds started—chains, engine, movement. The witness ran before seeing what happened next. The person who entered never came back out.

The entity has been given a name by locals: Rumage.

There’s no evidence of attacks outside the forest. No bodies, no blood trails, nothing. Just a boundary… and whatever waits on the other side of it.

This isn’t random. This isn’t human.

The forest isn’t just a location—it’s a line. And crossing it seems to be a death sentence.

I’ll continue documenting sightings and mapping the forest’s edge. Whatever Rumage is, it knows its territory… and it enforces it.

This case isn’t just about missing people anymore.

It’s about what’s waiting for them inside.

LOCAL POLICE DEPARTMENT
INCIDENT REPORT

Case Number: 85-07-119
Date Filed: July 14, 1985
Reporting Officer: Lt. R. Caldwell

SUBJECT: Suspicious Activity – Multiple Disappearances / Territorial Entity

SUMMARY OF INCIDENT:
This report follows multiple occurrences of individuals entering a wooded area locally referred to as the “Death Forest.” The location has earned its name due to a consistent and fatal pattern: no individual who enters the forest has been confirmed to return alive.

Current findings indicate that all incidents are confined strictly within the forest boundaries. No attacks or sightings have been reported outside of the area.

Evidence suggests the presence of a territorial hostile entity within the forest. The entity has been identified as “Rumage.”

Descriptions remain consistent: a humanoid figure with a blackened head, a glowing green smile, and a green-colored chainsaw. Witnesses also report the sound of dragging chains echoing through the forest.

It is believed that the entity does not pursue targets beyond the forest. However, any individual who enters its territory does not survive.

WITNESS STATEMENTS (ABRIDGED):

“My brother went into the forest, and I’ve never seen him again.”

“It stood at the edge… just watching. It didn’t move until someone stepped inside.”

“I heard chains and a revving sound, but it stopped the moment I backed away from the trees.”

OFFICER OBSERVATION:
Units dispatched to the forest perimeter report no direct encounters while remaining outside the boundary. However, multiple officers noted visual movement deeper within the tree line.

At approximately 02:13 AM, an officer stepped several feet past the forest boundary during a sweep. Audio recording captured the immediate onset of chain dragging and mechanical revving. Visual feed cuts shortly after.

The officer is currently listed as missing.

NOTES:

Entity “Rumage” appears strictly territorial and does not leave the forest.
All confirmed fatalities occur only after crossing into the forest boundary.
Entity may remain stationary or passive until entry is made.
Possible connection between Timmy’s investigation and the origin of the forest remains under review.
Behavior suggests awareness of boundaries and intentional enforcement of them.

STATUS: OPEN

RECOMMENDATION:
The area known as the “Death Forest” is to be treated as a restricted zone.

Civilians are advised to remain outside the forest at all times.

Crossing the boundary is considered a fatal action.

If you hear chains or mechanical sounds while outside the forest:

Do not approach
Do not enter
Maintain distance

It does not come to you.
You go to it.
