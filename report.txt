**LOCAL POLICE DEPARTMENT**
**INCIDENT REPORT**

**Case Number:** 85-07-119
**Date Filed:** July 14, 1985
**Reporting Officer:** Lt. R. Caldwell

---

**SUBJECT:** Suspicious Activity – Deceased Adult Male Reported Seen Walking

**NAME OF INDIVIDUAL:** Timmy [Last Name Unknown]
**AGE:** Approx. 32–38
**STATUS:** Confirmed Deceased

---

**SUMMARY OF INCIDENT:**
This report follows multiple late-night sightings of an adult male identified as “Timmy,” previously declared deceased. The subject was reportedly investigating a person of interest, William Afton, in relation to the disappearance of five children tied to an ongoing case.

According to prior records, Timmy had expressed suspicion toward Afton and was last seen attempting to gather evidence. Shortly after, Timmy was reported missing and later presumed dead under unclear circumstances.

In recent days, several witnesses claim to have seen a man matching Timmy’s description walking through isolated areas of town, including alleyways and near closed businesses. Witnesses consistently describe the subject as moving slowly, appearing disoriented, and failing to respond when addressed.

---

**WITNESS STATEMENTS (ABRIDGED):**

* “He looked like he was searching for something… or someone.”
* “I called his name, but he didn’t even turn his head.”
* “There was no way it was him… but it *was* him.”

---

**OFFICER OBSERVATION:**
Units dispatched to reported locations found no trace of the subject. Patrol logs confirm increased calls regarding similar sightings between 11:00 PM and 3:00 AM. No physical evidence has been recovered at this time.

---

**NOTES:**

* Subject was known to be actively suspicious of William Afton prior to death.
* Connection between Timmy’s investigation and current sightings remains unknown.
* Behavior reported does not align with normal human patterns.

---

**STATUS:** OPEN
**RECOMMENDATION:** Increase patrol presence in reported areas. Residents are advised not to approach the individual and to report sightings immediately.

---

**END OF REPORT**
